#### Purpose ####
Perform ls -lS (long ls with sorting by file size, displayed in descending order)
#### Usage ####

lss [options] [filename or directory name]

#### Options ####

-a include all hidden files (such as those beginning with .  and ..)
-A ignore hidden files (such as those beginning with . and ..)
-L ignore symbolic links
